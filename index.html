What I Do

BIM expertise across the project lifecycle

From concept models to coordinated, costed deliverables, here is how I add value.

Architectural & Structural Modeling

Full Revit models with floor plans, elevations, sections and isometric or axonometric views at LOD 300 to 400.

Reinforcement Detailing

Soldier piles, beam & slab framing, column schedules, stair and water-tank RCC detailing.

Parametric Families

Formula-driven, real-world components such as doors, windows, ceilings, joists and props, with 30 to 50+ parameters.

Dynamo Automation

Custom Python and visual scripts for quantity take-off and geometry joining that cut manual errors.

Scheduling & Cost Estimation

Room, door, window and material schedules, plus cement, brick and concrete take-offs exported to Excel.

Solar Study & Visualization

Sun-path analysis plus photoreal rendering in Enscape & Lumion to communicate design intent.
